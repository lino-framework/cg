========================
Lino Software Foundation
========================

The **Lino Software Foundation** is the non-profit legal entity who represents
the *Lino community* and acts as the :term:`product carrier` of the *Lino
framework*.

It does not yet exist; the whole work is currently done by :doc:`rumma`.
