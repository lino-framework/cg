====================
Lino Community Guide
====================

This website explains the :term:`Lino framework` as seen by the members of the
:term:`Lino community`.

.. glossary::

  Lino community

    The group of physical or legal persons who use the :term:`Lino framework`
    and adhere to the rules described in this guide when collaborating with
    other members. You may use the :term:`Lino framework` without being member
    of the Lino community. The legal entity representing the :term:`Lino
    community` is :doc:`rumma` as long as there is no :doc:`lsf`.
